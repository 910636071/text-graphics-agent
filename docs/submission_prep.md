## arXiv Endorsement Notes

arXiv requires endorsement before a first submission to arXiv or to a new
category. The endorsement flow starts during article submission; if endorsement
is required, arXiv sends an email with a request link or code.

As of the January 21, 2026 arXiv policy update, an institutional email address
alone is no longer sufficient for new authors. New submitters generally need
either:

- an institutional email plus previous arXiv authorship in the relevant
  endorsement domain, or
- personal endorsement from an established arXiv author in the same endorsement
  domain.

Endorsement is not peer review. The request should ask whether the paper is in
scope for the target arXiv category and appropriate to enter the submission
process.

Official references:

- <https://info.arxiv.org/help/endorsement.html>
- <https://blog.arxiv.org/2026/01/21/attention-authors-updated-endorsement-policy/>
